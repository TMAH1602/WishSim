## Intent

- Publish the Battle Test foundation, Yeoungin and Rimebound Benediction, the early three-tier ability system, smarter enemy tactics and poison, level-50 weapon projection, and four-star release companions Seo-yeon and Ji-ho.
- Preserve Ghostty as the primary supported terminal while retaining Kitty protocol and portable ANSI support.
